In the quiet town of Hanamura, where vending machines whispered secrets and the cherry blossoms never stopped falling, 17-year-old Kaoru discovered an old katana buried in the school’s forgotten storage room. The blade pulsed faintly — not with light, but with memory. Every night after that, Kaoru began dreaming of battles from centuries ago, fought by warriors who looked just like her friends. And every morning, the scar on her palm glowed a little brighter. Something ancient was waking... and the final battle might begin with a pop quiz on Monday.
